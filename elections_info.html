ANNOUNCEMENTS FROM THE ELECTIONS COMMITTEE

Primary Election will be held five weeks prior to Spring Break and only include presidential candidates. Presidential candidates that move on to the general election must register their Vice Presidential candidate with the Center for Student Invovlement two weeks prior to the general election. Primary election candidates who qualify for the general election may continue to table the weeks following the primary election.

QUESTIONS? CHECK OUT THE INFO BELOW!
 Any student is welcome to file and run for an elected SGA position. Check out the information below for help with how to file, how to run, and elections-related forms.

HOW TO FILE
The deadline to file is January 26, 2019 if you are filing for Student Body President, or February 16, 2019 for all other positions.
If you have any questions about the filing process or the SGA elections please call the Center for Student Involvement at 785-532-6541 or email us at studentinvolvement@ksu.edu.
For instructions on how to file, please visit the ksis help page available below. KSIS Filing Help Page
HOW TO RUN
Students who are interested in getting involved with Student Government should consider running the SGA General Election. In addition to running for Student Body President/Vice President, there are positions on Student Senate, Board of Student Publications, and the Union Governing Board that are open during each general election. The SGA General Election is held each spring, usually the first week in March. The process of running for an office can be a great experience by itself, and is not necessarily difficult. If you are interested in becoming involved, please take the time to carefully review all rules and regulations concerning expenditure reports, campaign materials, deadlines for forms, etc. Once you have received the Election Bundle and filed for a position, you will be responsible for upholding these rules and regulations. If you have questions regarding any of the election materials or the process of filing for a position, please send the Elections Committee your question.
