EXPLORE FUNDING OPPORTUNITIES BELOW
Forms are available for student organizations looking to request funds for on-campus or travel events. The form for Diversity Programming Committee funds is also available. These forms and many other useful forms are available in the OrgSync Portal: Resources for Student Organizations.

Diversity Programming Committee - DPC

Committee Chair: SGA Speaker Pro Tempore

Funding Cycle: Quarterly

Application Link
Description:
Are you a student organization looking to host an on-campus event to enrich appreciation or education of diversity? Apply for funding here! DPC oversees the allocation of money to student organizations for on-campus events for educational value that are intended to dissuade prejudice and discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, disability, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, geographic location, or age. Restrictions apply. Please see the application for more details.

Educational Opportunity Fund - EOF

Committee Chair: Student Body Vice President

Funding Cycle: Fall Semester

Application Link
Description:
EOF is limited to academic scholarships and fellowships, needs-based grants to those historically underrepresented in higher education, salaries or grants for students in community service programs, or salaries for students employed in campus student services. All departments and units of the University are eligible to apply.. A student organization can apply through a special consideration from the EOF committee or sponsorship through a department or unit. Funds allocated through the EOF are not to replace existing University support for financial aid and/or student services. Restrictions apply. Please see the application for more details.

Student Centered Tuition Enhancements - SCTE

Committee Chair: SGA Treasurer

Funding Cycle: Spring Semester

Application Link
Description:
Student Centered Tuition Enhancements proposals may be submitted to the committee in one of two areas: Programs classified as priority funding or Seed money for the creation and implementation of new programs/initiatives. Restrictions apply. Please see the application for more details.
