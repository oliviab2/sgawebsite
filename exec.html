BRANCH LEADERSHIP
Student Body President
Jordan Kiehl

jkiehl@ksu.edu | 114 K-State Student Union

The Student Body President serves as the chief executive of the K-State SGA. The primary duty of the Student Body President is to represent and protect the rights and interests of the student body. The Student Body President is duly elected by the K-State student body and serves a one-year term.

Student Body Vice President
Lacy Pitts

lacyp@ksu.edu | 114 K-State Student Union

The Student Body Vice President works to advance the agenda of the Student Body President and serves as the official representative of the student body in the absence of the Student Body President. The vice president is duly elected alongside the president and also serves a one-year term.
